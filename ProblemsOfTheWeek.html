Points

There will be points awarded to people who got the right answer and there may be points that are awarded to people who did not get the problem correctly but submitted a solution which was partially correct. There will be a leaderboard. The points from previous weeks will be worth less in the leaderboard that will contain the results of the next week's and of weeks that will be after that but they will still be worth an amount of points which may help you go up the leaderboard or at least not go down to the very bottom if you do not do the problems in one of the week (they decay by 50% of what they used to be each week). The level 1 problems are worth 5 points in the week that they are the current probems, the level 2 problems are worth 7, and the level 3 problems are worth 9 points. If a problem is submitted on a week after they are the current problems, they will not be worth any points.
